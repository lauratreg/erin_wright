About Erin

Hi, I'm Erin Wright, a Walla Walla based photographer that specializes in natural light family photography. I first became interested in photography when my oldest daughter was born and I was desperate to capture all those precious fleeting moments. I bought my first "nice camera" and set out to master a new hobby. I took several photography courses and just kept practicing! A couple of cameras and four kids later and people began asking me to capture their family as well! Soon my business was born! I absolutely love being able to provide families with images of their lives! Whether it's your big wedding day or a normal Saturday at the park with your kiddos, I would love to help you capture this season of your life!
